# Acme Horizon Workplace Conduct Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy describes expected workplace behavior at Acme Horizon.

## Scope
This policy applies to employees, contractors, interns, vendors, and visitors in offices, remote meetings, company systems, travel, and company-sponsored events.

## Applicability
Everyone working with Acme Horizon is expected to act professionally, respectfully, and honestly. Managers are expected to model and enforce these standards.

## Conduct Standards
Workers must treat others with respect, avoid harassment or discrimination, protect confidential information, and follow safety and security rules. Retaliation against someone who raises a concern is prohibited.

## Attendance and Availability
Employees should follow their approved schedules and notify their manager as soon as practical when they will be absent or late. Repeated unapproved absences may lead to corrective action.

## Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose outside work, gifts, relationships, or financial interests that could affect business judgment. Gifts over $100 from vendors or customers require manager and HR review.

## Approval Requirements
Outside employment that may overlap with Acme Horizon work time or customers requires manager and HR approval. Speaking publicly on behalf of Acme Horizon requires Communications approval.

## Exceptions
Reasonable accommodations, protected leave, and legally protected activity are handled under applicable policies and laws.

## Escalation and Contact
Concerns should be reported to a manager, HR at hr@acmehorizon.example, or the Acme Horizon ethics hotline listed on the intranet.

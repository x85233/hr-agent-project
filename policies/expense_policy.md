# Acme Horizon Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy defines how Acme Horizon employees request reimbursement for reasonable business expenses.

## Scope
This policy applies to employee business expenses, including meals, mileage, supplies, software, and approved home office items. It does not apply to contractor invoices.

## Eligibility
Full-time and part-time employees may submit eligible business expenses. Contractors may only submit reimbursable expenses if their contract allows it.

## Allowable Expenses
Common reimbursable expenses include approved travel meals, customer meeting costs, parking, business mileage, required software, and approved office supplies. Personal purchases, fines, entertainment without a business purpose, and family travel are not reimbursable.

## Approval Requirements
Expenses under $250 require manager approval. Expenses from $250 to $999 require manager and department head approval. Expenses of $1,000 or more require director approval before purchase. All expenses require itemized receipts.

## Submission Timeline
Employees should submit expenses within 30 days of the purchase date. Expenses submitted after 60 days may be denied unless HR or Finance approves an exception.

## Reimbursement Limits
Home office accessories are reimbursable up to $300 per calendar year with manager approval. Business meals are capped at $25 for breakfast, $35 for lunch, and $60 for dinner unless a director approves an exception.

## Exceptions
Client emergencies, accessibility needs, or travel disruptions may justify higher expenses. Employees should document the reason and seek approval as soon as possible.

## Escalation and Contact
Questions about expense eligibility should go to Finance at finance@acmehorizon.example. Disputes may be escalated to the employee's manager and HR.

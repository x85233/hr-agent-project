# Acme Horizon Data Security Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy describes basic data security expectations for Acme Horizon employees and contractors.

## Scope
This policy applies to all Acme Horizon workers, systems, files, messages, devices, and third-party services used for company work.

## Applicability
All employees, contractors, interns, and temporary workers must protect company, employee, customer, and vendor data. Managers are responsible for making sure their teams understand these requirements.

## Data Handling Requirements
Company data must be stored only in approved systems. Confidential HR data, payroll data, customer records, and security credentials must not be downloaded to personal devices or shared through personal email.

## Device and Access Rules
Workers must use multi-factor authentication for company systems. Company laptops must use disk encryption, approved endpoint protection, and automatic screen lock after 10 minutes of inactivity. Passwords must not be reused or shared.

## Remote Work Security
Remote workers must use trusted networks and the company VPN when accessing internal systems. Public Wi-Fi may only be used with VPN enabled.

## Approval Requirements
New software that stores company data requires Security review before use. External sharing of confidential data requires manager approval and a valid business reason.

## Exceptions
Security may approve exceptions for urgent business needs, but exceptions must include an expiration date and compensating controls.

## Escalation and Contact
Suspected data exposure, lost devices, phishing, or unauthorized access must be reported to security@acmehorizon.example immediately.

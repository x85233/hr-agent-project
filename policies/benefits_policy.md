# Acme Horizon Benefits Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy summarizes benefits eligibility and enrollment rules for Acme Horizon employees.

## Scope
This policy applies to U.S. employees of Acme Horizon. Benefit plan documents control if there is a conflict with this sample policy.

## Eligibility
Full-time employees working 30 or more hours per week are eligible for medical, dental, vision, life insurance, disability coverage, and the retirement plan. Part-time employees working 20 to 29 hours per week have limited benefits eligibility and may enroll in dental, vision, and the retirement plan. Contractors are not eligible for employee benefits.

## Enrollment
New eligible employees must enroll within 30 days of their hire date. Coverage begins on the first day of the month after hire unless plan documents state otherwise. Employees may change elections during open enrollment or after a qualifying life event.

## Medical, Dental, and Vision
Acme Horizon offers medical, dental, and vision options for eligible employees and eligible dependents. Employee contribution amounts are listed in the annual benefits guide.

## Retirement Plan
Eligible employees may enroll in the 401(k) plan after 30 days of employment. Acme Horizon provides a company match of 50% on the first 6% of eligible pay contributed by the employee.

## Leave and Benefits Continuation
Benefits may continue during approved paid leave if employee contributions are paid. Benefits during unpaid leave are handled by HR according to plan rules and applicable law.

## Approval Requirements
Benefit elections do not require manager approval. Late enrollment, retroactive changes, or exceptions require HR Benefits review.

## Exceptions
Qualifying life events may include marriage, divorce, birth, adoption, loss of other coverage, or a dependent eligibility change. Documentation may be required.

## Escalation and Contact
Employees should contact benefits@acmehorizon.example for enrollment questions or HR for eligibility disputes.

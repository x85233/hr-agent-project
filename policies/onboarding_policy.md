# Acme Horizon Onboarding Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy describes the onboarding process for new Acme Horizon employees.

## Scope
This policy applies to new hires, rehires, and internal transfers starting a new role. Contractors follow a separate onboarding checklist.

## Applicability
Managers, HR, IT, and new hires share responsibility for completing onboarding steps on time.

## Pre-Start Requirements
New hires must complete employment forms, background check steps, and benefit acknowledgments before their start date when possible. IT should prepare standard equipment before the employee's first day.

## First Week Expectations
New employees should attend HR orientation, review core policies, complete security training, and meet with their manager to confirm role expectations. Managers should provide a 30-day onboarding plan.

## Training Requirements
All new employees must complete data security, workplace conduct, and anti-harassment training within 10 business days of hire. Role-specific training deadlines are set by the manager.

## Approval Requirements
Onboarding delays longer than 5 business days require manager and HR review. Early system access before the official start date requires HR, IT, and manager approval.

## Exceptions
Urgent business needs, accommodation requests, or delayed background checks may require an adjusted onboarding schedule.

## Escalation and Contact
New hires should contact their manager for role questions, HR at hr@acmehorizon.example for paperwork, and IT at it@acmehorizon.example for access or equipment.

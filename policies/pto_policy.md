# Acme Horizon PTO and Leave Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy describes how Acme Horizon employees accrue, request, and use paid time off, holidays, and sick leave. It is sample content for an HR policy assistant project and does not describe a real company.

## Scope
This policy applies to Acme Horizon employees in the United States. State or local leave laws may provide additional protections.

## Eligibility
Full-time employees are eligible for paid time off after their first day of employment. Part-time employees working at least 20 scheduled hours per week receive prorated PTO. Contractors, interns, and temporary agency workers are not eligible for Acme Horizon PTO.

## PTO Accrual
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of PTO per calendar year during years 0 through 3. Employees with 4 or more years of service accrue 20 days per year. Part-time eligible employees accrue PTO based on scheduled hours. PTO does not accrue during unpaid leave.

## Holidays and Sick Leave
Acme Horizon observes 10 company holidays each year. Full-time employees receive 8 sick leave days per year. Part-time eligible employees receive prorated sick leave. Sick leave may be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or care for an immediate family member.

## Approval Requirements
PTO requests of 1 to 5 business days require manager approval. Requests longer than 5 consecutive business days require manager and HR approval. Employees should submit PTO requests at least 10 business days in advance when practical.

## Carryover and Payout
Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Unused sick leave does not pay out at year end. PTO payout at separation follows applicable state law and Acme Horizon payroll procedures.

## Exceptions
Emergency leave, protected leave, bereavement leave, jury duty, and military leave may follow separate rules. HR may approve exceptions when required by law or documented business need.

## Escalation and Contact
Questions should be sent to the employee's manager first. Policy exceptions or disputes should be escalated to HR at hr@acmehorizon.example.

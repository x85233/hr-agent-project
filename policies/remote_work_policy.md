# Acme Horizon Remote Work Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy explains remote and hybrid work rules for Acme Horizon. It is fictional sample content for development and testing.

## Scope
This policy applies to employees whose roles can be performed remotely. Some roles require on-site work because of customer, security, or equipment needs.

## Applicability
Hybrid eligible employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require director and HR approval. Contractors must follow the remote work terms in their contract.

## Work Location Requirements
Employees must work from an approved U.S. location unless HR and Legal approve otherwise. International remote work is not allowed without written approval from HR, Legal, Security, and Payroll at least 30 days before travel.

## Availability and Communication
Remote employees must be available during core collaboration hours from 10:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. in their assigned office time zone. Employees must keep their calendar current and respond to urgent manager messages promptly.

## Equipment and Workspace
Employees must use approved company devices for company work. Home workspaces should be private, secure, and suitable for confidential conversations.

## Approval Requirements
Hybrid schedules require manager approval. Remote work outside the employee's assigned state for more than 10 business days requires HR review. Any international work requires written approval before work begins.

## Exceptions
Temporary remote work may be approved for weather events, office closures, medical needs, or family emergencies. Acme Horizon may modify or end remote work arrangements based on business needs.

## Escalation and Contact
Employees should contact their manager for schedule questions and HR at hr@acmehorizon.example for location or accommodation questions.

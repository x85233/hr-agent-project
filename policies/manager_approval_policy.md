# Acme Horizon Manager Approval Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy defines common approval levels for HR, finance, travel, equipment, and work arrangement requests.

## Scope
This policy applies to approval workflows used by Acme Horizon employees and managers.

## Applicability
Employees are responsible for submitting accurate requests. Managers are responsible for reviewing requests promptly and documenting decisions.

## Approval Matrix
PTO requests of 1 to 5 business days require manager approval. PTO requests over 5 business days require manager and HR approval. Expenses under $250 require manager approval. Expenses from $250 to $999 require manager and department head approval. Expenses of $1,000 or more require director approval before purchase.

## Remote Work Approvals
Hybrid schedules require manager approval. Fully remote roles require director and HR approval. Remote work from another U.S. state for more than 10 business days requires HR review. International remote work requires HR, Legal, Security, and Payroll approval.

## Equipment Approvals
Standard equipment follows the onboarding process. Non-standard equipment under $500 requires manager approval. Requests of $500 or more require department head approval.

## Travel Approvals
Domestic business travel requires manager approval. International business travel requires director approval and Security review before booking.

## Exceptions
Emergencies may be approved after the fact when advance approval was not practical. The employee must document the reason and submit the request within 5 business days.

## Escalation and Contact
Approval disputes should be escalated to the next-level manager. HR can help interpret policy requirements at hr@acmehorizon.example.

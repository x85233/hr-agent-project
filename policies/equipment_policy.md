# Acme Horizon Equipment Policy

## Purpose
This synthetic policy explains how Acme Horizon provides, tracks, and reimburses work equipment.

## Scope
This policy applies to company-issued laptops, monitors, phones, accessories, and approved home office equipment.

## Eligibility
Full-time employees receive standard equipment based on role. Part-time employees receive equipment required for their scheduled work. Contractors receive equipment only when approved by the contract owner and Security.

## Standard Equipment
Standard equipment may include one laptop, one charger, one headset, and one external monitor. Sales and executive roles may receive a company mobile phone when approved by a director.

## Reimbursement Limits
Employees may request reimbursement up to $300 per calendar year for approved ergonomic or home office accessories. Chairs, desks, and specialty equipment above this amount require HR and manager approval.

## Approval Requirements
Standard equipment is approved during onboarding. Non-standard equipment under $500 requires manager approval. Equipment or reimbursement requests of $500 or more require department head approval.

## Returns and Asset Tracking
Company equipment must be returned within 5 business days after employment ends or when requested by IT. Lost, stolen, or damaged equipment must be reported to IT within 24 hours.

## Exceptions
Accessibility accommodations may require additional equipment. HR and IT will review accommodation requests and may approve exceptions to standard limits.

## Escalation and Contact
Employees should contact it@acmehorizon.example for equipment issues and HR for accommodation-related equipment questions.
